#### **Survey Template**



Title \& Abstract - Concise Title \& 150 to 250 words (summarized view)

Introduction : Background, Significance and scope of the survey

Methodology : Description of selection criteria and classification

Thematic body section : sections are clustered by methodologies or topics

Discussion : Analysis of patterns, contradictions, and leading research

Open issues and future directions : specific research recommendations

Conclusion : Summary of discussion and the overall significance of the survey

References
